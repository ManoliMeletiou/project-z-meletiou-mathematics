## Mandatory first diagnostic

Every student completes an adaptive starting diagnostic before the main game unlocks. It is the game prologue, not a grade. Confirm pathway/cohort and accommodations; teach inputs; adapt through prerequisites/targets; detect misconceptions; allow pause/resume; weight guessing, hints and revisions appropriately; stop only at sufficient confidence or an explicit inconclusive result. Then show strengths, priority foundations, first mission and why. Never shame, publicly rank or permanently restrict learning.

No diagnostic item serves without approved curriculum placement, independent answer verification, misconception mapping, accessibility review and qualified human mathematics review. Calibrate placement against teacher-reviewed cases.

## Game and learning design

Course = universe; domains = worlds; prerequisite clusters = regions; skills = stages; teach/guided/independent/application/review activities = missions; mixed checks = checkpoints; multi-skill transfer tasks = boss challenges; enrichment = side quests; companion growth reflects healthy learning.

XP, streaks, levels, achievements, companion states and cosmetics motivate only; they are never grades. Reward independent reasoning, correction, explanation, spaced review and perseverance. Use compassionate streak recovery. No loot boxes, paid randomness, public shaming, manipulative scarcity or pay-to-win.

Every active skill must support at least **2,000 genuinely distinct, reproducible, verified instances** across meaningful families: conceptual/procedural, representations, reverse/error analysis, familiar/unfamiliar/authentic contexts, difficulty, calculator boundaries, misconceptions and applicable assessment demands. Validate parameters; independently recompute answers; test equivalence, domains, units, precision, notation, distractors, duplicates, ambiguity and accessibility; require human approval and quarantine regressions.

The AI tutor inspects attempts, diagnoses likely misconceptions/prerequisites, chooses a representation/smaller step, gives the least revealing hint, asks the learner to act, checks reasoning, repairs misconceptions, summarizes the idea and gives an independent check. It does not simply reveal answers. Ground it in approved content; apply child safety, injection resistance, privacy, audit, limits and teacher escalation; evaluate learning gain.

## UX, roles and security

Each primary page has one dominant next action, at most two visible secondary actions and advanced tools behind progressive disclosure. Make location, purpose and next action obvious. Build WCAG 2.2 AA keyboard, focus, screen-reader, reduced-motion, contrast, responsive and recovery states.
